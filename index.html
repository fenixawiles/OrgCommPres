UTSA & UT Health San Antonio Integration

A concise guide to the merger’s purpose, benefits, and where to explore deeper details.

The Big Picture

The UTSA–UT Health San Antonio merger creates a unified research university that combines health sciences, engineering, clinical care, and public service. This integration strengthens statewide healthcare delivery, accelerates research, and expands academic pathways for students across Texas.

Use this overview to navigate the focused pages on economic outcomes, statewide impact, city and regional growth, student experience, faculty research, and quantitative data.

  • Research Scale: R1 designation, NIH-funded centers, and cross-disciplinary labs.
  • Health Delivery: 60+ patient care sites, 2.5M+ visits, and expanded clinical trials.
